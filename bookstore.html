The Reading Grounds



Business Description
The Reading Grounds is a combination bookstore/coffee shop where customers can browse books and have coffee and snacks. We will hold regular Book Club meetings as well as Poetry and Children's Story readings.


Coffee:

  1. Latte (Iced & Hot)
  2. Cappuccino
  3. Cold Brew (Vanilla, Caramel, & Hazelnut)

Tea:
  1. Black Tea (Earl Grey & Chai)
  2. Green Tea (Jasmine & Mint)
  3. Herbal Tea (Hibiscus & Lemongrass

Snacks:
  1. Cookies (Chocolate Chip & Oatmeal)
  2. Cakes (Carrot & Tiramisu)
  3. Croissants (Chocolate & Almond)
